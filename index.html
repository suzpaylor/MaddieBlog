What I Do

I am a designer and artist who loves to paint, design prints, costumes, children's and women's wear. I have spent time working in production, product of development, fashion design teams and visual merchandising. I am currently designing silk scarves in Adobe Illustrator to help raise funds for Operation Underground Railroad. I enjoy painting and am inspired by scenery when designing my clothing.

Who I Am

I grew up in Salt Lake City, Utah. I love to spend much of my time exploring new places. I have always been an artist and eventually combined my love for art with fashion. I love everything from the outdoors in Utah to the energy of New York City. I am currently teaching yoga and believe in a healthy lifestyle. My graduation show was inspired by different flowers found while hiking, European art and ballet. I enjoy painting with water colors and oils. I recently finished my degree at the University of Utah in Communications with an emphasis in PR, Marketing and Advertising. Two years prior I graduated from SLCC in Fashion Design and Merchandise. While in school I spent time doing fashion internships in New York and Salt Lake. I love helping others. I am currently designing silk scarves for Operation Underground Railroad to help raise funds for their organization. I look forward to continuing work in the fashion industry though out my life.
